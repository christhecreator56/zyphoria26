🌟  About the Symposium
Zyphoria '26 is an International Symposium that brings together students, innovators, and
technology enthusiasts from various institutions. It encourages creativity, innovation, and
collaboration through engaging competitions.
Core Pillars
Innovation: Experience cutting-edge technology showcases, AI-driven challenges, and
hands-on competitions curated by iDataMind and CSE faculty.
Competition: Battle the brightest minds across institutions in 14+ technical and non-
technical events.
Community: Connect with industry experts, researchers, and fellow tech enthusiasts from
institutions worldwide.
